How Verification Works

Every facility. Every visit. Documented.

Smart QR codes turn every entry point, restroom, and common area into a verification checkpoint — simple for technicians, transparent for you.

01 / SCAN

QR Checkpoint

Technicians scan a posted QR code to open the work order for that exact location.

02 / DOCUMENT

Inspect & Capture

Photos, GPS coordinates, and timestamps are attached automatically as the work is completed.

03 / AI REVIEW

Automated Screening

Submissions are screened for missed trash, floor condition, restroom cleanliness, and safety hazards.

04 / VERIFY

Supervisor Sign-Off

A human supervisor reviews every AI-flagged report before it's marked complete.

05 / REPORT

You See It

Your dashboard updates instantly — what was done, where, when, and by whom.
